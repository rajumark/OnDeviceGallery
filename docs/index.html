A Note From The Developer

Created to solve the problem of my life - struggling to find specific photos quickly without internet access or compromising my privacy. This app was designed to put the power back in your hands, keeping everything 100% on your device.

I built OnDeviceGallery to make photo management effortless and completely private. By making it open source and free, I hope others can benefit from this solution and even improve it. Feel free to contribute, report issues, or make it even better for everyone. Together, we're creating a better, more privacy-focused way to manage our digital memories.
